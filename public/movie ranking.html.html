The Top Movies According to Pandora

My top movies of 2023

Oppenheimer I

The story of American scientist J.
Robert Oppenheimer and his role in the development of the atomic bomb.

Past Lives

Nora and Hae Sung, two deeply connected childhood friends,
are wrested apart after Nora's family emigrates from South Korea.
Twenty years later, they are reunited for one fateful week as they confront
notions of love and destiny.

Poor Things

The incredible tale about the fantastical evolution of Bella Baxter;
a young woman brought back to life by the brilliant and unorthodox scientist,
Dr. Godwin Baxter.
